How to Cook Meth (Sugar Candy)

Don't worry guys, this is how to cook a blue sugar candy... or smth.

  1. Bring some sugar and blue food coloring
  2. Set the frying pan and heat it up
  3. Put the sugar into the frying pan until it melts
  4. Then, put some blue coloring into your melted sugar
  5. Mix it until the color is evenly blended
  6. Pour the hot mixture into your favorite mold
  7. Let it cool down — congrats, 99.1% pure blue meth... candy 🍬
